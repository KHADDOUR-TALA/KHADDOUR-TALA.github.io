Humanitarian Work

Syrian Earthquake Relief - Jableh

February 2023 - Community Support Volunteer

In the aftermath of the devastating earthquake, I joined relief efforts in Jableh to assist displaced families. My work focused on:

  • Coordinating shelter and basic needs distribution
  • Providing emotional support to affected families
  • Organizing community activities for children
  • Assisting in medical aid distribution

This experience reinforced my belief in the resilience of the human spirit and the importance of community. It strengthened my commitment to developing technology that serves humanity in times of need.
