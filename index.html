Professional Summary

I am a Robotics and Intelligent Systems Engineer with focused expertise in rehabilitation robotics, intelligent control systems, and human–robot interaction. My research integrates mechanical design, control theory, and computer vision to develop assistive technologies that enhance human capability and accessibility.

My engineering approach is fundamentally human-centered, addressing real-world challenges in healthcare and rehabilitation contexts. The motivation for my work is shaped by the complex needs of resource-limited environments and post-conflict recovery scenarios, where technological innovation must balance sophistication with practicality, durability, and cultural appropriateness.
